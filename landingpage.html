About Us

Johnson Hospital is a non-stock, non-profit Healthcare Institution. It is licensed by the Department of Health and accredited by the Philippine Hospital Association and Philippine Health Insurance Corporation. It has (6) six major clinical departments and recognized by the different specialty societies for its Residency Training Program for Department of Internal Medicine, Pediatrics, Surgery, Obstetrics and Gynecology. MJH is also accredited as Mother-Baby Friendly Hospital.
